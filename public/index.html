A New Way of Learning

Everyone learns differently, which is why we have a broad range of dials and switches to make sure you can learn the way you learn best.

You can choose between lectures, articles, interactive activites or a combination of these to optimise your learning.

You can choose for us to remind you, or even force you, to have a break, and you could even play some of our inbuilt games when taking a break.
